Art & Craft

The decades old rich tradition of creativity and art needs no introduction when it comes to Jammu and Kashmir. Kashmir's specialties include carpets, papier-mâché products, Pashminas, Copperware and Silveware.Kashmir is a treasure trove of arts and crafts. Kashmiri carpets, shawls, and silverware are recognized worldwide for their fine quality and craftsmanship. Check out our Cheap Kashmir tour packages where you can visit shop Kashmir Hadicrafts.

Zaeka-e-Kashmir

In Jammu, the dishes of Dogra cuisine are quite popular. This region is famous for rajma chawal, Dishes like Ambal, Khatta Meat, Kulthein Dal, Dal Patt, mark the prominence of Dogra cuisine in Jammu. The other traditional dishes that are a must-try in Jammu Region are Morel (Gushi) Palov, Madra, Oria, Maani, Khameera, Katha Meat, and and Mitha Bhat. As desserts, you cannot overlook the street food of Jammu comprising Gol Gappas, Kachaalo, Gulgule, and Rajma Kulche.
